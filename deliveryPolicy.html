  • Shipping costs are applicable to all customers.

Shipments Outside USA

X-Scorpio items, if shipped outside of the USA, may be subject to import duties and taxes. X-Scorpio does not pay these duties and taxes and you are not charged when ordering. These costs will be invoiced by the shipping carrier upon delivery of your items and you will be responsible for paying such import duties and taxes.

Customs and tax rates vary from country to country and also depend on the items that are shipped. We have no control over these charges and encourage you to contact your local customs office for more information before placing your order. The COD service is currently only available in GCC countries.
